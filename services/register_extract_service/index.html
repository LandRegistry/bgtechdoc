Register Extract Service

Overview

Use this service to get source data from the Land Register in an electronic data stream (alongside the usual supply of the official copy).

Find information on:

  • the property
  • the proprietor
  • any leases or restrictions that apply to the property
  • any agreed notices, unilateral notices, cautions or charges that apply to the property

Our quick reference indicator shows entries that may need further action for your clients.
